Control Mapping

Physical Controls and Their Digital Equivalents

Use this mapping to explain cybersecurity decisions in business language during budget, governance, and contract discussions.

Locked doors

Digital equivalent: hardened endpoints and identity access controls.

Alarm system

Digital equivalent: managed SOC monitoring, alert triage, and response support.

Visitor logs

Digital equivalent: centralized event logging and SIEM visibility.

Key inventory

Digital equivalent: privileged account control and periodic access review.

Fire drill

Digital equivalent: incident response playbooks and tabletop exercises.

Asset inventory

Digital equivalent: endpoint, SaaS, and cloud asset inventory with backup validation.

Decision Model

When to Prioritize SOC

  • You need continuous monitoring and incident response support.
  • You want lower incident detection time and less operational disruption.
  • Your team needs regular triage and containment guidance.
